# PlantPal Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026
**Developer:** Loopspur

## Summary
PlantPal does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All plant data is stored locally on your device.

## Data Collection
PlantPal collects **zero** user data. Specifically:
- No personal information (name, email, phone)
- No location data
- No device identifiers
- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No crash reporting data sent externally

## Data Storage
All data (plants, watering schedules, health logs, settings) is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services
PlantPal uses **no** third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The app works entirely offline.

## In-App Purchases
PlantPal offers a one-time premium upgrade through Google Play Billing. Purchase information is handled entirely by Google Play and is subject to Google's privacy policy.

## Photos
If you add photos to your plants, they are stored locally on your device. Photos are never uploaded or transmitted.

## Data Export
PlantPal allows you to export your data as a JSON file. This export is saved to your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Children's Privacy
PlantPal does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since PlantPal collects no data from anyone, this is inherently satisfied.

## Changes
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in the app and on this page.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy: support@loopspur.com
