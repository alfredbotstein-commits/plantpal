🌿 **PlantPal — Water on time. Every time.**

Tired of plant care apps that charge $36/year just to remind you to water? PlantPal is different. One-time purchase. No subscription. Ever.

**Why PlantPal?**

🔔 **Reliable Reminders** — Local notifications that actually fire. No server dependency, no missed alerts. Your plants get watered on time, every time.

🌱 **500+ Species Database** — From Monstera to Snake Plant, we know exactly how often each species needs water. Summer and winter schedules included.

📱 **Dead Simple** — Add a plant in under 30 seconds. Name it, pick the species (or skip), set your schedule. Done. No accounts, no sign-ups, no sync complications.

💧 **Satisfying Watering** — Mark plants as watered with a delightful animation. Build your watering habit with joy, not guilt.

📅 **Calendar View** — See your entire watering schedule at a glance. Know exactly which plants need attention today, tomorrow, and next week.

📊 **Health Tracking** — Log waterings, repottings, pest treatments, pruning, and notes. Keep a complete care history for every plant.

🌙 **Dark Mode** — Beautiful in any light. Automatic or manual dark mode support.

**Free Features:**

**Premium ($2.99 one-time):**
